Concept /

Sacred hot spring water/five times concentrated/Rich in mineral ingredients
＝ECLEAD Premium Series

“Eclead premium series” contains rich mineral ingredients (Metasilicic acid, Metaboric acid, Bicarbonate ion, Radon etc.) by mainly using Kotohogi hot spring water which penetrates into the stratum corneum with its fine particle composition. We carefully sourced special water and concentrated five times to extract natural mineral ingredients. We blended another 70 different components for beautification (Placenta extract, Hyaluronic acid, Ceramide, Nicotinamide mononucleotide etc.) After opening, product should be used within 30 days to maximize the benefit of these components.

Sacred Kotohogi hot spring water has fine particle clusters

＜ECLEAD＞

Eclead uses Kotohogi hot spring water with fine particle clusters, 1/30 size of tap water which carries and penetrates beauty ingredients to the stratum corneum.

＜Others＞

If coarse-grained particle water (tap water, purified water) is used for the products, ingredients stay on the surface of the skin without penetrating.

Experience the feel of ultimate moisture on your skin

Kotohogi hot spring water concentrated five times

The hot water used to create Eclead products are yielded from Kashihara, spiritual center of Nara prefecture. Kashihara shrine named it as sacred hot spring water.

Our unique technology enabled us to concentrate this hot water 5 times, extract more natural mineral components. This Kotohogi hot spring water from the land where spirituality and nature coexist will rejuvenate your skin.
