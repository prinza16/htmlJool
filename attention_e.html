Instructions and directions for use

  • Not to use when you have injury, rash on your skin.
  • Stop using when you experience redness, itchiness and irritation on your skin.Consult with dermatologist as soon as possible.
  • Take care so this product does not get into your eyes, if this happens, wash well with water and consult with a doctor if you feel irritation.
  • Mineral components may crystallize and white lines may appear inside the bottle, but there is no problem with the quality. Shake lightly before use.

Storage and handling

  • Close the cap tightly after each use, keep out of reach of children.
