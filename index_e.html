We use specially sourced Kotohogi hot spring water for “Eclead” premium series, located near Kashihara Shrine, Nara. Three sacred treasures of Eclead

The Imperial Family inherited the three ancient sacred treasures of Japan. The Kasihara shrine is where the first emperor was enshrined. Following a ceremony at the shrine, the name of the sacred hot spring water Kotohogi was chosen. This Kotohogi water is the main ingredient of Eclead and provides precious quality for all of our products. Our premium series are created in this spirit.
